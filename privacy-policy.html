AfriLoad Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 7 June 2026

This privacy policy applies to the AfriLoad mobile application (the "Application"), operated by Dickson Muoki Wambua (the "Service Provider"). By installing or using AfriLoad, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download, install, and use it. This includes:

  • Personally identifiable information you provide: Name, Email address, Phone number, Profile photo, National ID or driver's license image (for verification), Vehicle registration documents
  • Location data (current and approximate, used for matching loads to nearby transporters)
  • Device information (model, operating system, language)
  • Internet Protocol address
  • Application usage data, including pages visited, time spent on those pages, and date/time of visits

How We Use Your Information

The Service Provider uses collected information to:

  • Authenticate users and maintain their accounts
  • Verify identity for trust and safety (document verification)
  • Match brokers with transporters for freight services
  • Process and route payments through M-Pesa
  • Improve application performance and user experience
  • Communicate important notices, service updates, and (where permitted) marketing communications

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Application or its third-party SDKs may use cookies, SDKs, pixels, and similar technologies to support functionality, analytics, or service delivery. Where required by applicable law, the Service Provider will obtain consent before using non-essential tracking technologies.

Location Information

The Application collects your device's location to provide location-based features such as finding loads near you, specifying pickup and delivery addresses, and calculating distances for routing. Location data may be shared with third-party mapping services used to support these features.

Artificial Intelligence

The Application uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies — including services provided by OpenAI — to enhance user experience and provide certain features such as content recommendations and automated assistance. AI processing may involve transmitting limited user data to AI service providers. All AI processing is performed in accordance with this privacy policy and applicable laws.

Third Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services, each with its own privacy policy:

  • Google Play Services — Android system services
  • Expo — application framework and build tooling
  • Supabase — authentication, database, and storage backend
  • OpenAI — AI-powered features
  • Sentry — crash and error reporting
  • RevenueCat — subscription and purchase management
  • Safaricom M-Pesa (Daraja) — payment processing for users in Kenya

Third Party Access

The Service Provider may share your information with third parties:

  • As required by law (subpoena, court order, or similar legal process)
  • When the Service Provider believes in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect rights, safety, investigate fraud, or respond to government requests
  • With trusted service providers who work on the Service Provider's behalf and have agreed to adhere to this privacy policy

Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services to aid in improving the Application.

International Data Transfers

The Service Provider or its third-party service providers may transfer personal data to countries outside your country of residence, including outside the European Economic Area (EEA). Where applicable law requires safeguards for international transfers, the Service Provider will use appropriate mechanisms such as:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions or other legally recognized transfer mechanisms
  • Your consent, where required and legally permitted

Kenya Data Protection Rights

This Application is operated by a service provider based in the United States, with operations focused on the Kenyan market. AfriLoad complies with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 ("KDPA") with respect to personal data of Kenyan residents.

If you are a Kenyan resident, you have the following rights under the KDPA:

  • Right to be informed about the use of your personal data
  • Right of access to personal data the Service Provider holds about you
  • Right to object to the processing of your personal data
  • Right to correction of false or misleading personal data
  • Right to deletion of false or misleading personal data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Right to data portability — receiving your data in a structured, commonly-used format
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact the Service Provider at zawadilabs@gmail.com. The Service Provider will respond within the timeframes required by the KDPA.

If you believe your data has been mishandled and you are not satisfied with the Service Provider's response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) of Kenya at https://www.odpc.go.ke.

Personal data of Kenyan users may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Kenya, including the United States (Supabase auth and storage, OpenAI services), Singapore (Supabase database region), and other countries where service providers operate. Where such transfers occur, the Service Provider applies safeguards as required by the KDPA, including appropriate contractual protections.
