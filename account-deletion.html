AfriLoad — Account Deletion

How to delete your AfriLoad account and associated data

AfriLoad ("we", "us", "our") is committed to giving users control over their personal data. This page explains how to delete your AfriLoad account, what data is removed, what data we may retain, and how long the process takes.

Account deletion is a permanent action. Once your account is deleted, you will not be able to recover it, your load history, your bid history, your messages, your ratings, or any other data associated with your AfriLoad profile.

How to delete your account

Option 1: In-app deletion (preferred)

  1. Open the AfriLoad app
  2. Sign in to your account
  3. Go to Profile (bottom navigation)
  4. Tap Settings or your profile icon
  5. Scroll down and tap Delete account
  6. Confirm by entering the OTP sent to your registered phone number
  7. Your account deletion request is queued for processing

Option 2: Email request

If you cannot access your account or prefer to request deletion by email, contact us:

Email: zawadilabs@gmail.com
Subject line: Account Deletion Request

Please include the following in your email:

  • The phone number registered with your AfriLoad account (e.g., +254712345678)
  • The name on the account
  • A brief statement confirming you are the account holder and you wish to delete the account

We will respond to your email within 7 business days to confirm we have received your request, and your account will be processed for deletion within 30 days of confirmation.

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed from our active systems:

  • Your profile information (name, photo, phone number, email if provided)
  • Your verification documents (ID, driver's license, vehicle registration if you uploaded them)
  • Your active load listings (if you are a shipper)
  • Your active bids (if you are a driver)
  • Your in-app messages
  • Your saved locations and preferences
  • Your notification settings
  • Your device information and analytics tied to your account

What data is retained

Important: Certain data may be retained after account deletion for legal, financial, and platform integrity reasons, as permitted by the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019. This data is anonymized or pseudonymized where possible.

We may retain the following data for the periods indicated:

  • Transaction records — Any completed freight contracts, payments, and associated invoices are retained for a minimum of 7 years to comply with Kenyan tax law, financial regulations, and dispute resolution requirements. These records are anonymized where possible (e.g., your name removed, transaction ID retained).
  • Ratings and reviews you wrote about others — Reviews you posted about other users may remain visible (without your name attached) to preserve the integrity of those users' reputations on the platform.
  • Fraud and abuse records — If your account was associated with fraud, scams, or platform abuse, certain records are retained indefinitely to prevent the same individual from re-registering and harming other users.
  • Aggregated analytics — Anonymous statistical data (e.g., "how many loads were posted in Nairobi in March") may be retained indefinitely. This data cannot be linked back to you.
  • Server logs and security records — Standard server access logs and security event records are retained for up to 90 days for security and debugging purposes.

How long does deletion take

30-day processing window. From the date you submit a deletion request (in-app or via email), we will complete the deletion of all eligible data within 30 days.

Typical timeline:

  • Day 0: You submit the deletion request
  • Day 1 to 7: We confirm receipt of your request and verify your identity
  • Day 7 to 30: We process the deletion across all systems (active database, backups, third-party services)
  • Day 30: Deletion is complete; you will receive a final confirmation email

In rare cases (for example, if you have pending freight contracts that are mid-delivery), we may delay deletion until those obligations are resolved, but never more than 60 days. You will be notified if your request is delayed.

Third-party services

AfriLoad uses third-party services to deliver functionality. When you delete your account, we also request deletion or anonymization of your data from these providers, subject to their own retention policies:

  • Supabase (database and authentication provider) — Your account record, profile, and associated data are deleted from Supabase.
  • Safaricom M-Pesa (payment processor) — We delete the link between your AfriLoad account and your M-Pesa identifier. M-Pesa's own retention policies apply to transaction records under Safaricom's terms.
  • Sentry (crash reporting) — Crash reports tied to your device or account are deleted within 90 days.

If you change your mind

Once your account is fully deleted, we cannot restore it. However, within the first 14 days of submitting your deletion request, you may contact us at zawadilabs@gmail.com to cancel your request. After 14 days, deletion proceeds and is irreversible.

Your rights under the Kenya Data Protection Act

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data (as described on this page)
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner of Kenya: https://www.odpc.go.ke
